    Jungle street lives

    It's been a few weeks now, but the inspiration and buzz is still lingering. a higlight of my career was introducing Susan Kare at a Dnbbble + Photoshop meetup at Adobe's headquaters in San Franscisco. Last year, Rich, Charles Pearson and some of the photoshop team had a braintorm meeting about doing another event together. I thew out something that time, seemed like a pipe dream and/or a long shot. "Hey, let's ask Susan Kare to give a short tolk. "I had just watched a video of a presentation she gave and it was incredibly compelling.Miracuously, she said yes, even creating custom icons for the event. She went on to speak for 45 minutes about her journey as an artist and her pioneering work as the original icon and typeface designer for the originam Macintosh. Her early work solidified many concepts that we all still see today: The wath, paint bucket, trash can, save etc as well as font and interface systems that inspired everything else that followed them.
